Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: January 2026

How to Delete Your Data from AiLy

Your Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of all your personal data and app data from AiLy at any time.

Option 1: Delete Your Account (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete all your data is to delete your account:

  1. Open the AiLy app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to Account Settings
  4. Click Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion

What gets deleted: When you delete your account, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account information (email, name)
  • All your app data (grocery lists, recipes, storage items, expenses, meal plans)
  • Your preferences and settings
  • All associated data in our database

Option 2: Request Data Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email:

  1. Send an email to: aily@gmail.com
  2. Subject: "Request to Delete My Data"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your email address associated with the account
    • Your name (if available)
    • Confirmation that you want to delete all your data
  4. We will process your request within 30 days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once your data is deleted

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Immediate: Your account will be deactivated and you will not be able to log in
  • Within 30 days: All your data will be permanently deleted from our servers
  • Backups: Data in backups will be deleted during the next backup cycle (within 90 days)
  • Cannot be recovered: Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered

Data Retention Exceptions

We may retain certain information if required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

  • Legal obligations (tax records, etc.)
  • Dispute resolution
  • Fraud prevention

However, this retained information will not be used for any other purpose and will be deleted as soon as legally permitted.

Facebook Login Users

If you signed in using Facebook, you can also revoke access through Facebook:

  1. Go to your Facebook account settings
  2. Click Apps and Websites
  3. Find "AiLy" in the list
  4. Click Remove to revoke access

Note: Revoking Facebook access will prevent you from logging in, but to fully delete your data from our servers, you still need to delete your account using one of the methods above.

Google Login Users

If you signed in using Google, you can also revoke access through Google:

  1. Go to your Google account settings
  2. Click Security
  3. Under "Third-party apps with account access", find "AiLy"
  4. Click Remove to revoke access

Note: Revoking Google access will prevent you from logging in, but to fully delete your data from our servers, you still need to delete your account using one of the methods above.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about data deletion or need assistance, please contact us at:
