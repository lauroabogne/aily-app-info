Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

AiLy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AiLy application and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address: Used to identify your account and send important updates
  • Name: To personalize your experience

Your account is secured using industry-standard encryption. We use secure authentication methods to protect your account access.

2.2 Social Login (Google/Facebook)

If you sign in with Google or Facebook, we only receive:

  • Your email address
  • Your name

Authentication is handled entirely by Google or Facebook. We do not have access to your Google or Facebook account credentials.

2.3 App Data

We store the data you create in the app:

  • Grocery lists and items
  • Recipes and cooking instructions
  • Storage items and expiration dates
  • Expenses and budgets
  • Meal plans
  • Your preferences and settings

This data belongs to you and is only accessible by you.

2.4 Usage Information

  • Device information (to ensure compatibility)
  • App usage patterns (to improve the app experience)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and sync your data across your devices
  • Provide you with the app features and services
  • Send you important account-related notifications
  • Improve our app based on how you use it
  • Respond to your questions and provide support
  • Keep your account secure

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our database. We use industry-standard security measures:

  • Account Security: Your account is protected using industry-standard encryption methods
  • Data Encryption: Data is encrypted when transmitted between your device and our servers
  • Access Control: Access to your data is restricted to authorized systems only
  • Backups: Your data is backed up regularly to prevent data loss

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted services to help us provide the app:

  • Firebase Authentication: Helps us securely handle login with Google and Facebook
  • Google Sign-In: Allows you to sign in with your Google account
  • Facebook Login: Allows you to sign in with your Facebook account

These services only provide us with your email address and name. They have their own privacy policies that you can review on their websites.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may only share information if:

  • You explicitly ask us to
  • Required by law or legal process
  • To protect the safety and security of our users

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • View and update your account information anytime
  • Export your data
  • Delete your account and all your data at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

8. Data Retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete all your personal information and app data, except where we are legally required to keep certain records.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Please review this policy from time to time.
