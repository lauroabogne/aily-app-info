What is AiLy?

AiLy is a complete home management solution designed to help you organize and manage all aspects of your household in one convenient app.

Features

  • Grocery Lists: Create and manage your shopping lists with ease
  • Recipes: Store and organize your favorite recipes with cooking instructions
  • Storage Inventory: Track items in your storage with expiration dates
  • Meal Planning: Plan your meals ahead of time
  • Expense Tracking: Monitor your household expenses and budgets
  • Notifications: Get reminders for expiring items and grocery lists

Our Mission

We aim to simplify home management by providing a comprehensive, user-friendly solution that helps you stay organized and save time.
