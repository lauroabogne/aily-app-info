Send Feedback

We value your feedback! Your input helps us improve AiLy and make it better for everyone. Please share your thoughts, suggestions, or report any issues you've encountered.

📱 In-App Feedback

The easiest way to send feedback is directly from the AiLy app:

Settings → Send Feedback

This method allows you to include app version and device information automatically, which helps us diagnose issues faster.

📧 Email Feedback

You can also send feedback via email:

aily@gmail.com

Please include as much detail as possible, such as:

  • What feature or issue you're reporting
  • Steps to reproduce (if it's a bug)
  • Your device type and app version
  • Screenshots (if applicable)

💡 Types of Feedback We Welcome

  • Bug Reports: Issues or errors you've encountered
  • Feature Requests: Ideas for new features or improvements
  • User Experience: Suggestions to make the app easier to use
  • Performance: Reports of slow performance or crashes
  • General Feedback: What you like, what could be better

🙏 Thank You!

We read every piece of feedback and take your input very seriously. Your feedback directly influences our development priorities and helps us build a better app for everyone.
