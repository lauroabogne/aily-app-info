Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
You can create an account by signing up with your email address, or by using Google or Facebook login for quick access.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your account information and app data are stored securely and are only accessible by you.
Can I sync my data across multiple devices?
Yes, your data is automatically synced across all devices where you're logged into your account.
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from the Settings page in the app, or by contacting us at lauroabogne@gmail.com. See our Data Deletion page for more information.
How do I get notifications for expiring items?
Enable notifications in the app Settings. You can customize how many days in advance you want to be notified about expiring storage items.
Can I export my data?
Yes, you can export your data at any time. Contact us at lauroabogne@gmail.com to request a data export.
Is the app free to use?
Yes, AiLy is free to use. All core features are available without charge.
How do I report a bug or issue?
You can report bugs or issues by using the "Send Feedback" feature in the app, or by emailing us at lauroabogne@gmail.com.
What platforms is AiLy available on?
AiLy is available on Android and iOS mobile devices, as well as web browsers.
How can I contact support?
You can contact us via email at lauroabogne@gmail.com or use the Contact Us page in the app. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
